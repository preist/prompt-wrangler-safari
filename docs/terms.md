# Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 9, 2024**

## Privacy Policy

### No Data Collection
Prompt Wrangler does not collect, store, transmit, or record any user data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

### Local Processing Only
- All sensitive data detection and anonymization occurs entirely on your machine
- No data is sent to external servers or third-party services
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
- No user accounts, registration, or authentication required

### Data Storage
The extension does not store any of your prompts, sensitive data, or browsing activity. Any processing is done in real-time and immediately discarded.
